Who are we?

About us

We're Ridge Computing, a nonprofit organization created by a group of high schoolers dedicated to inspiring the next generation of coders.

We firmly believe that coding is a valuable skill that anyone should be allowed to learn. Thus, we hope to make computer science more accessible to everyone.

Why us?

All of our proceeds are used in order to help the community!


Our diverse courses are tailored to make learning easy for students of all levels!


Our instructors have a variety of qualifications, from high rankings in USACO and HackerRank to extensive portfolios on GitHub!

What courses do we offer?

Courses

Our online courses teach a variety of programming skills. You can learn anything from fundamental concepts in Java to creating a model with Keras.

Our courses are entirely online, and cost 50 USD to register for. All of the money from these courses will be used to help fund local businesses.

We also have workshops on a variety of topics such as Git, Docker, and Kubernetes planned. Stay tuned for more information regarding these events!
